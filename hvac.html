FAQs

Common HVAC questions.

How often should air-conditioning be serviced?

Most units should be inspected and cleaned every 3 to 6 months depending on usage, dust, and site conditions.

Can you repair industrial HVAC equipment?

Yes. Controltek supports diagnostics, component checks, servicing, and repair coordination for commercial and industrial HVAC systems.

Do you provide maintenance plans?

Yes. Preventive plans can cover coil cleaning, drainage checks, gas checks, electrical inspection, and performance reports.
