About Controltek

Reliable engineering support for buildings, energy systems, and climate control.

Controltek Services Limited is a Lusaka based technical services company built around dependable workmanship, disciplined safety practices, and practical engineering solutions. Our teams support clients across Zambia with installation, diagnostics, repairs, preventive maintenance, and project delivery.

From air-conditioning systems and solar installations to electrical works, plumbing, and building construction, we bring structured planning, skilled technicians, and clear communication to every site.
