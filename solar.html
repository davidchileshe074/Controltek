FAQs

Common solar questions.

How do I know the right inverter size?

The inverter size depends on your running load, startup load, and which appliances must operate during outages.

Do batteries need maintenance?

Battery systems should be checked for health, wiring condition, ventilation, settings, and operating logs.

Can solar reduce generator use?

Yes. A correctly sized hybrid system can reduce generator runtime and provide cleaner backup power.
